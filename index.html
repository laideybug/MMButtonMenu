Mile Mapper

Walk. Run. Explore.

Mile Mapper creates random routes for walking or running! Simply select the distance that you want to travel and Mile Mapper will build you a loop that starts and ends at your chosen location.

Free

Mile Mapper is free to download and use. If you like it and want to contribute to its ongoing development, consider donating with the in-app Tip Jar.

Independent

Built by an independent team of one, Mile Mapper is a labour of love. I hope you'll find it as useful as I do.

Responsive

A stripped-down flat design featuring a bespoke, open source menu control. If you've got coding chops, feel free to contribute!

Mix up your running routine and explore your environment

Discover new routes and get to know your surroundings. Keep your running plan fresh and stay motivated for longer.

Understand your route before you even step outside

View step-by-step distance and elevation data. Weather forecast data ensures you won't get caught in the rain.

View your history and keep your favourites

Build a library of your favourite routes to access anytime.
